Ducks

OBJECTIVE

To animate a character walking across the screen.

PROGRAM USED

Adobe After Effects, Illustrator

OUTCOME

I chose to create a scene with ducks instead of people as I wanted to experience animating an animal. Ducks of different colours, sizes and states were made to create a typical setting you would see by a lake. Sound effects were added to make the scene more realistic and relaxing.
