OBJECTIVE

To design a logo for a scavenger hunt themed marketing app. The logo must include a panda, have some relationship with Vancouverâ€™s Chinatown area. This logo is to be turned into a series of stickers.

PROGRAM USED

Adobe Illustrator

OUTCOME

A well known food component of Chinatown includes steamed char-siu buns. As the app targets a younger audience, Stampii was rendered as a cute panda to attract this demographic and have a similar affect to that of Puglie Pug. A simple colour palette was used to consider printing capabilities on different printers and materials.
