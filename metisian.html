Metisian

In Collaboration with Carmen Lockhart

OBJECTIVE

To design a visual identity for a local potter that portrays their ethnic roots and values in pottery.

PROGRAM USED

Adobe Illustrator

OUTCOME

The brand identity is comprised of aspects of Métis and Japanese culture, both part of Carmen’s heritage. The two main elements describe balance between the two backgrounds. This design was kept monochromatic in order to be utilized in applications such as a signature stamp. Separate elements in the signature include: arrowheads, a symbol of the Métis and Carmen’s family line; the belt of Orion, a symbol of a family business; and a Whiskey Jack, the bird associated with the Swampy Cree.
