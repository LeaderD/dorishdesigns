About Me

Hi! My name is Doris Ho, and I'm a designer based in Vancouver, BC. I graduated from Langara College's Design Formation program in 2017. I'm currently expanding my skillset at BCIT in their Digital Design and Development program. After graduation I will be pursuing a career in motion graphics while completing my Bachelor's in Business Administration.

Aside from doing design, one of my favourite things to do is watch cartoons. My favourite shows include She-Ra and the Princesses of Power, Steven Universe, Teen Titans (the original series), Bee and Puppycat, and The Dragon Prince. I also enjoy drawing and writing my own original stories. One of my lifelong goals is to write a comic and create an animated action series fully using After Effects. I love teaching, and hope that once I get more industry experience, that I will be able to teach motion graphics in a post secondary environment.
